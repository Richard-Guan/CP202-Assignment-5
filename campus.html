Waterloo Region Food Reviews

Campus
Pizza

Campus Pizza is located in Waterloo plaza and offers the ultimate student budget meal, pizza. They offer student-friendly meal combos of a slice of pizza and a drink for $4.25, which is hard to beat. They’re also open from 10am-5am every single day, which will be convenient for those late studiers and those who have late night cravings. However, you get what you pay for at Campus Pizza.

 

Despite the fact that one slice of pizza is absolutely huge, the quality is not on par with all the pros Campus Pizza has to offer. If you were to order a single slice pizza, it will usually be quite dry, cold and greasy. Although the taste will not be amazing, it will certainly be filling enough to curve your hunger. We give Campus Pizza a 7/10!
