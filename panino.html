Mr.Panino's

Mr. Panino’s used to be an Italian restaurant until new owners took over and rebranded the restaurant as Mr. Panino’s Beijing House. Since the restaurant’s transition from Italian cuisine to Chinese cuisine, the establishment has been successful ever since. The reason for their success is Mr.Panino’s being able to better serve the large Asian demographic within close proximity to the University of Waterloo.

 

Mr. Panino’s food is questionable from time to time as students have mixed reviews about them. Some students report that on certain days the food could taste adequate, however some days the food could give you a bad stomachache. Despite the risk of eating Mr. Panino’s, students still go back every single year. This is due to their ability to serve home-cooked meals that allow students to reminisce about their past childhood and for this reason we give Mr. Panino’s a 5/10.
