Foodie
Fruitie

Foodie Fruitie is located next to Waterloo university and is a relatively new establishment to appear in Waterloo’s food repertoire. That being said, its’ décor is very modern and open, providing a very welcoming and clean atmosphere. Foodie Fruitie is a popular location among the student population for great, authentic Asian cuisine. They offer ramen, bubble tea, BBQ pork, and many other local favourites. Their menu selection is huge and their variety allows for a selection of foods for any time of the day.

 

Not only is the food great, the prices are affordable and reasonable. You can get a dinner set with salmon, rice and soup for only $10! They also offer complementary seaweed salad appetizer and ice cream for dessert after the meal. The only downside is the service. During busy periods, it takes some time to get assigned a table and it takes even more time to flag down a server once you’re ready to order. All in all, we would give Foodie Fruitie a rating of 9/10!
