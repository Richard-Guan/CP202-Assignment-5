Itamae
Sushi

Itamae is right at the heart of king and university. It is a buffet style sushi restaurant with a spacious interior and modern design. Being a buffet style restaurant, the price range will be higher than a normal sit-down location, however the price is decent considering what you’re paying for. Unfortunately, Itamae has had a rough history in the past, however upon visiting them recently, their food quality has seemed to have improved.

 

You get a large selection, as expected from a buffet restaurant, including sushi, sashimi, meat, noodles or rice and desserts. The order is easily placed on an iPad and the food is delivered to you quite quickly with friendly servers. As I mentioned before, the food quality has improved compared to a couple of years ago, however it’s still not stellar. The rice can be dry and often times, the quality of the meat and fish are noticeably not on par. With that being said, we give Itamae Sushi a 6.5/10!
