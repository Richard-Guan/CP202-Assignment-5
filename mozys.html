Mozy's

Mozy’s Shawarma is a definite crowd pleaser, with a seemingly constant line at all times of the day. Mozy's services large crowds with assembly-line efficiency, all while ensuring delicious food quality, speed, and service. Their garlic sauce is amazing, their meats are well barbequed and juicy and the plate is big enough for two meals.

 

They offer a standard and humble selection of toppings, and they go a bit further and allow the guilty pleasure of adding fries or rice in their wraps. Not only is their service and food quality excellent, their meals are affordable as well. One could argue there's better choices for pure quality, but Mozy's is great all around and in a prime location for all students to enjoy. With that all being said, I give Mozy’s a 9.5/10!
