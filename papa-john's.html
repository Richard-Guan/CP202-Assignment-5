Waterloo Region Food Reviews

Papa
John's

Papa Johns is an American pizza chain branching out to Canada. The Waterloo location is situated right next to Laurier. There is minimal standing area as you are meant to just order and go. The pizza is great for people who love a greasy pizza but for many others, it may be too oily. The toppings are fresh and good quality and the dough is never too dry or too heavy.

 

There are many other food options for those who want more variety, such as chicken wings, cheesy bread sticks, and even desserts. In terms of service, the workers are usually polite, courteous and try their best to get your food in your hands as fast as possible. I give Papa Johns a 7/10!
